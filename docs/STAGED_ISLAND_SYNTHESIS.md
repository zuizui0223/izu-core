# Staged island synthesis

This analysis begins with transcribed observations from the supplied Inoue-series papers.
It compares four declared scenarios:

- environment_only
- body_size_only
- small_bee_substitution
- ardens_bridge_loss

The evidence currently included is estimated outcrossing and common-garden flower-length summaries. It does not include spot traits, public occurrences, climate, genomic structure, direct handling, or a dated history.

The ordinal island chain is a sensitivity scaffold only. It is not a time axis.

## Next public data channels

1. occurrence records for plant and bee groups, with source, date, coordinates, and identification quality;
2. island climate, area, isolation, elevation, and land cover;
3. photo-derived spot presence, fraction, and position with image-level uncertainty;
4. taxonomy and phylogeny/genomics;
5. documented pollination interactions and sampling effort.

Occurrence is modeled as availability evidence and cannot be treated as a direct estimate of pollen transfer or past absence.
