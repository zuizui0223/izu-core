## Why v1.1 is retired

The v1.1 amendment was based on an unfinished image-analysis output from the
companion `shimahotarubukuro` work. That analysis was not the user-declared final
dataset or final analysis. Later segmentation and quality-control work does not
change this boundary: unfinished guide work stays outside current evidence rather
than being retained as a provisional biological result.

Accordingly, v1.1 supplies none of the following:

- an adopted island mean;
- an adopted Oshima-to-no-Bombus direction;
- an adopted breakpoint or second transition;
- a validated nectar-guide phenotype;
- a causal pollinator result; or
- an independent holdout observation.

## Re-entry rule

The visible-signal channel can enter a future contract only after the user declares
a final dataset and analysis. That future version must specify the sampling unit,
exclusions, trait definition, preservation treatment, aggregation rule, uncertainty,
and intended biological interpretation. It must be versioned as a new contract and
must not silently overwrite either v1.0 or this retired v1.1 audit record.
