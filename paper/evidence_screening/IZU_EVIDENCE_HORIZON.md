# Izu evidence horizon: expand data, not infrastructure

## Why this table exists

The evidence problem is no longer a missing workflow. The current direct
geographic evidence is concentrated in *Campanula* and is insufficient for a
claim about broad parallel change across the Izu flora. This table separates
three things that must not be inflated into one number:

1. **Primary geographic lineage channels**: direct mainland--island or
   ordered-island comparisons that could enter the main synthesis after source
   checking.
2. **Auxiliary comparative channels**: taxon/history/context sources that may
   explain a pattern but cannot become within-species replicates by default.
3. **Blinded photo cohorts**: existing image cards that can create new ordinal
   trait data after prespecified, blinded scoring; photo availability alone is
   not a trait measurement.

The companion CSV contains 20 concrete channels. It is not a claim of 20
independent effects.

acquisition map showing the fastest honest route toward that scale.

## Acquisition order

### Wave 1: recover five original studies

1. *Ligustrum ovalifolium* -- direct qualitative direction already verified;
   recover trait tables, locality labels, n, variation and visitor effort.
2. *Weigela coraeensis* -- direct design confirmed by title; recover traits,
   direction, population map and numeric summaries before assigning an effect.
3. *Campanula punctata* -- recover crossing-trial denominators and locality
   mapping.
4. *Lilium auratum* -- resolve taxonomic and population design before treating
   varieties as a geographic comparison.
5. *Chionographis japonica* -- verify the reported Kozushima comparison in the
   original text before it enters any primary tier.

### Wave 2: score the six existing blinded photo cohorts

The source bundle contains 99 cards: *Ajania pacifica* (25), *Angelica
keiskei* (7), *Farfugium japonicum* (21), *Peucedanum japonicum* (10),
*Pittosporum tobira* (23), and *Rubus trifidus* (13). Score a single
predeclared, visible floral-display trait per species before revealing region.
Record image phase, focal flower visibility, trait visibility and exclusion
reason. Only scoreable cards create C-rank evidence.

### Wave 3: use the two Hydrangea cohorts only after balanced cards exist

Current records establish island-proxy photo availability, not phenotype. Do
not score or claim a geographic trait pattern until mainland and island
flowering-phase cards are blinded together.

## Decision rule

Do not add another source-discovery workflow until Wave 1 has either recovered
or closed all five original-study targets and Wave 2 has produced a scored
photo table. After that point, the data will tell us whether to expand the
cohort, collect field measurements, or write the paper as a systematic
source-graded evidence map rather than a quantitative meta-analysis.
